About MolToxNet

MolToxNet is a curated collection of chemical compounds with associated toxicity data, aimed at advancing research in toxicity prediction and environmental health.

Dataset Overview

  • Data Size: Over 10,000 unique compounds with toxicity measurements.
  • Tasks: Classification, regression, and multi-task predictions for various endpoints.
  • Applications: Drug safety, environmental chemistry, regulatory compliance.
